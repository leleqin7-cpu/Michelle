Admission Requirements · 申请要素

Know What It Takes
to Get In

TOP 10 & Ivy
GPA 3.8+ · TOEFL 110+ · SAT 1540+ / ACT 34+
Academic Depth
  • International competition awards (IMO, IOI, ISEF, REGENERON STS, USAMO)
  • Publications in high-quality journals (IEEE, Nature, CS conferences)
  • Highly selective summer programs (RSI, SSHI, TASP, SUMAC, ROSS, PROMYS)
Leadership & Impact
  • Founder or core leader of multiple communities/organizations
  • Driving real-world change (policy, public interest, tech products, social enterprise)
Unique Identity
  • Cultural background, family story, or growth trajectory with strong recognition
TOP 30
GPA 3.7+ · TOEFL 108+ · SAT 1500+ / ACT 32+
Academic Ability
  • National or state-level academic competitions
  • Independent research projects with mentor guidance or published results
Activity Depth & Leadership
  • Founded or led at least 1 distinctive activity (nonprofit, app, community project)
  • Core roles in other activities demonstrating sustained commitment and influence
TOP 50
GPA 3.5+ · TOEFL 100+
Academic Extension
  • Proactive extracurricular learning (university MOOCs, EDX/Coursera, independent reading, Online High School)
  • Clear academic interest direction with visible exploration process
Activity Participation
  • Active in campus/community clubs, volunteer service, interest groups
  • Sustained engagement in at least one area (music, sports, debate, STEM, writing)
